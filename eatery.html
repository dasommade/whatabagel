What an Eatery

We've taken our love for bagels and extended our bakery to include a full service brunch restaurant; From omelettes, eggs any style, specialty sandwiches, renown shakshua, freshly squeezed juices, cheese blintzes, French toast and pancakes, and fantastic coffee, we certainly have something for everyone. The Eatery takes great pride in its heaping portions and its commitment to the freshest and highest quality ingredients that leave our customers satisfied.
