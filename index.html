ABOUT & HISTORY

At What A Bagel we make sure our bagels are fresh and warm all day, every day. What's our secret? It's simple - Every bagel is made in-store, straight from our ovens and into your hands. We mix the dough from scratch, boil them, dress them, bake them, and serve them to you all in minutes.

What a history

What A Bagel takes three generations of bagel making experience, and compacts them into every bite. Richard Farbman, the founder of What A Bagel, worked with his father for over 10 years in the wholesale bagel industry. In 1997, the vision was set out. Richard took everything he knew about the wholesale bagel-making process and great customer service and packed it in to a single retail store.

Today,there are twelve What a Bagel locations through out the GTA,and growing. Richard's sons operating the three corporate locations, and with the six franchises, what a bagel is a fast growing business. With What a Bagel's unparalleled bagel production, it's clear that three generations of bakers produce the perfect recipe for bageling success.
